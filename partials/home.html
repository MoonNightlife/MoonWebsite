Share With Your Friends

Sharing your plans for the night has never been easier. With a tap of a button you can let your friends see where you are going, so they can decide to tag along. Say goodbye to going out by yourself.

Explore New Venues

Moon allows you to view the hottest venues in your area. Never find yourself going out to a place where no one is attending. Don't want to travel far? With Moon's mapping feature you can find the hottest bars that are closest to you.

Get The Deals

Tired of paying for overpriced drinks? Well look no further, Moon has you covered by supplying the most popular specials in you area all in one convenient spot.

Moon Nightlife: A Elegant & Simple iOS App

An app that helps you plan a complete night out in the city.

Sneak Peak of Moon

Moon's Features Include:

  • Share with your friends where you are going that night. Personalize your profile to show your favorite drink and bar!
  • See where your friends are going that night (check out Moon's View)
  • See how many people are going to certain night clubs and bars. Search for a bar and checkout its profile to see who is going.
  • See social events in your city
  • See which bars have the best specials that night
  • Check out the top bars that night based on how many people are going
  • Use our specialized map to see which areas of the city are the most popular that night.
  • Moon is currently available in Dallas
